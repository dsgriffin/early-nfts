0xmons Fully Onchain In 2021

Only 87 0xmons had their GAN animations put onchain in 2021, making them the first known onchain AI artworks.
While some other 0xmons had static images of their artwork put onchain in 2021, this list focuses only on full animations.
The first AI NFT to include both art AND the algo/neural network onchain is XOR by Nahiko, released on 9th August 2021. With that in mind, 0xmons put onchain before this date could be of extra interest.
